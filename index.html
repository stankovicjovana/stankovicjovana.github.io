About Samoyeds:

The Samoyed is a breed of large herding dog, from the spitz group, with a

  • thick,
  • white,
  • double-layer coat.

It takes its name from the Samoyedic peoples of Siberia. These nomadic reindeer herders bred the fluffy white dogs to help with the herding. To find out more about the breed click on the Wiki page.
